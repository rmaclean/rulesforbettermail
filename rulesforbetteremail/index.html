Rules for better mail

The Problem

Email is out of control! It takes too long to respond to and results in continuous inflow for everyone.

Some solutions propose a discipline to dealing with email but that can lead to unintended consequences.

The Solution

A few simple rules to improve your email?

  • Be disciplined - only write what you need to! That maybe three lines, it maybe three hundred.
  • Be honest & understanding. Email lasts, you don't want it to bite you later.
  • Put the people that must action the email in the TO line & those that just need a copy of the email, CC them.
  • Have more than one point that needs an action? Number the points for easy conversation.

Rules for better email is a personal policy. If you find a situation that it fails in, do not apply it.
